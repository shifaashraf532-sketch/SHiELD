Project Description
SHEild is a high-impact mobile safety tool designed to provide immediate emergency response and automated legal assistance for women. It bridges the gap between mobile software and emergency services to ensure help is always accessible.
The Problem Statement
Women often face situations where they cannot safely or quickly reach for their phones to call for help. Additionally, many lack immediate access to formal legal procedures or information regarding their fundamental rights during an emergency.
The Solution
We solve this by implementing an integrated software suite featuring Shake-to-SOS technology and a digital AI Legal Guide. The app allows users to trigger silent alerts through physical movement or UI interaction and provides instant FIR drafts to bridge the gap between an incident and legal action.

Features
Feature 1: Silent SOS: Sends a high-priority emergency SMS with current status to a pre-set trusted contact.
Feature 2: Shake-to-Alert: Monitors the device accelerometer to detect high-impact movement and trigger an SOS automatically.
Feature 3: Safe-Taxi Monitor: Real-time tracking that alerts contacts if a vehicle deviates from the intended safe path.
Feature 4: AI Legal Guide: A dedicated module that provides instant access to legal rights and generates automated formal complaint drafts.
